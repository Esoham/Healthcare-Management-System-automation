Feature: Patient Registration

  Scenario: Successful patient registration
    Given I am on the patient registration page
    When I enter the following patient details:
      | First Name | Last Name | Date of Birth | Gender | Email           | Phone      | Address           |
      | John       | Doe       | 1990-01-01    | Male   | john@example.com | 1234567890 | 123 Test Street   |
    And I click the register button
    Then I should see a registration success message

  Scenario: Patient registration with missing information
    Given I am on the patient registration page
    When I enter the following patient details:
      | First Name | Last Name | Date of Birth | Gender | Email           | Phone      | Address |
      | Jane       |           | 1995-05-05    | Female | jane@example.com | 9876543210 |         |
    And I click the register button
    Then I should see error messages for the missing fields
