Feature: Appointment Scheduling

  Scenario: Successful appointment scheduling
    Given I am on the appointment scheduling page
    When I enter the following appointment details:
      | Patient ID | Doctor ID | Date       | Time     | Type       |
      | P12345     | D67890    | 2024-09-01 | 14:30:00 | Check-up   |
    And I click the schedule button
    Then I should see an appointment confirmation message

  Scenario: Appointment scheduling with unavailable time slot
    Given I am on the appointment scheduling page
    When I enter the following appointment details:
      | Patient ID | Doctor ID | Date       | Time     | Type       |
      | P12345     | D67890    | 2024-09-01 | 10:00:00 | Check-up   |
    And I click the schedule button
    Then I should see a message indicating the time slot is unavailable

  Scenario: Appointment scheduling with invalid patient ID
    Given I am on the appointment scheduling page
    When I enter the following appointment details:
      | Patient ID | Doctor ID | Date       | Time     | Type       |
      | INVALID    | D67890    | 2024-09-01 | 11:00:00 | Check-up   |
    And I click the schedule button
    Then I should see an error message for invalid patient ID
